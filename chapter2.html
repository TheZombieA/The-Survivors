Other Survivors?

And to their surprise, the gun worked and it teleported them far enough to run away into the wilderness ; but , that wasn’t the end of the story, because they had nowhere to go and hide , and they were very tired , so they had to find somewhere to call home , but that wouldn’t be easy , because there were zombies scattered around the whole city , and they couldn’t do anything about it , unless they would fight with all of their power , but even then two people wouldn’t be able to end dozens of zombies by themselves , and they didn’t know if there were any survivors other than them , but they didn’t have any other options , because they were very well unprepared , and that can cause serious injuries or even death if they even dared to try . So that was it , they decided to sleep the night in a hidden hallway , and right as the sun rised , they started looking in every place possible trying to find if there were any survivors other than them , they spent hours even days just looking and looking without finding a trace or just even a clue that can lead into thinking there were any other survivors , but right as they were going to forget about this mission , they heard sounds screaming for help , as the sound got closer , they saw their classmates Jake and Lisa being attacked by zombies , so they got their gravity guns out , used them on the zombies wish changed their gravity to zero , took their friends and run as fast as they could because they knew that they couldn’t stay any longer or they would be in big trouble. Jake says while being traumatized : Oh boy ! I don’t know what we could’ve done if you guys didn’t help us out … Emma cuts him out while saying : Forget about that , most importantly now is that we know we aren’t alone in this monstrosity of a city .
